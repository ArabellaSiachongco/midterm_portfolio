About

I've transformed my life, and I believe you can do the same.
I discovered comfort in exploring manhua, but at a certain point, I felt a sense of longing while witnessing others pursue their dreams. Recognizing these feelings, I made a conscious decision to break free from the cycle of uncertainty and embarked on a journey towards personal growth. Amidst the challenges and self-doubt, I chose to embrace positivity as my guiding mantra. This shift in mindset has allowed me to find genuine joy in letting life's unpredictable waves shape my future.

In 2022, I took a significant step forward by enrolling at the University of the Cordilleras, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Web Technology, while simultaneously participating in Kodego. This decision was fueled by my sincere passion for coding. Contrary to initial expectations, the University of the Cordilleras experienced delays in introducing basic HTML and CSS.
As a result, I learned through platforms like Free Code Camp, which turned out to be a valuable experience. While I consider myself a continuous learner, I approach challenges with enthusiasm, relying on YouTube tutorials to grasp concepts and applying them with minor modifications to suit my unique style.
Despite occasional procrastination challenges, I view them as opportunities for self-improvement. Each obstacle becomes a stepping stone towards honing my skills and overcoming any barriers in my educational journey. I will continue to navigate towards a future filled with bright possibilities.

Life is indeed a journey,
and each new day presents opportunities for both personal and professional growth, as well as moments of genuine happiness.
